₹ 2.29 Lakh

The KTM 250 Duke is a naked motorcycle in the 250cc segment. The overall design of the motorcycle is similar to its elder sibling, the 390 Duke. The front profile features a sleek headlamp design with boomerang-shaped LED daytime running lights.
